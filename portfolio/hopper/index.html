Hopper

JULY 2025

Optimizing generative AI for creativity.

Hopper is a platform that enables users to easily harness their creative visions and generate them using state-of-the-art AI image generation models.

  • The power of AI generated media in creative output use cases is underestimated because it is challenging and unintuitive to articulate your creative visions via a prompt to a model.
  • To address this, Hopper acts as a go-between, helping you construct your idea through focused conversation.
  • Hopper extracts this information and processes it into a refined, optimized prompt for Nano Banana Pro, and provides a built-in SDK to edit your image online.

I built Hopper in collaboration with Code and Theory, a New York City-based creative agency.
